# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We want MineSIer to be a constructive place for people learning programming,
Minecraft modding, and networking. Participate in good faith and be respectful
of people with different levels of experience.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate
disruption, publishing private information, and conduct that makes the project
unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may restrict
participation when behavior violates these standards. Report conduct concerns
privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Do not use public issues for
sensitive reports.
